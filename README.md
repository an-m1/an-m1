## About Me

Hi! I’m **Ankit Modhera**, a final-year **Computer Science** student at **York University** who enjoys building real-world software and intelligent systems. My interests span **full-stack development**, **backend systems**, and **robotics**, especially projects that combine **computer vision**, **embedded programming**, and **control logic**.

I’m drawn to projects where clean software design, strong problem-solving, and system-level thinking intersect, whether that’s a scalable web platform or robotics work involving **computer vision**, **embedded systems**, and **control logic**. I enjoy building maintainable systems, learning quickly, and collaborating in team environments.
